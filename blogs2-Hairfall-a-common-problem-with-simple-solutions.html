Hairfall - a common problem with simple solutions

Hair fall is a common problem faced by many individuals. There are "n" number of reasons for hairfall to happen. Dandruff, low serum calcium and d3, anemia, disturbance in liver metabolism, excessive masturbation, are some of few reasons.

Excessive use of oil and shampoo without knowing the reason for Hairfall can increase the same. Shampoo can cause inflammation in roots, while excessive oil can cause seborrhoeic dermatitis, and can also become a cause for dandruff.

You also need to evaluate your daily routine. Smoking chewing tobacco and drinking excessive alcohol, should be decreased gradually.

Ayurveda, the traditional Indian system of medicine, offers natural remedies for hair fall that are safe and effective. Here are some General Ayurvedic tips for preventing hair fall:

  • Amla: Amla or Indian gooseberry is a rich source of Vitamin C, which is essential for healthy hair growth. You can consume amla in various forms such as fresh fruit, juice or powder.
  • Bhringraj: Bhringraj or Eclipta Alba is an herb that is commonly used in Ayurvedic hair care products. It nourishes the hair follicles and helps prevent hair fall.
  • Coconut oil: Coconut oil is a natural hair conditioner and helps to nourish and strengthen the hair. Regular massaging of the scalp with warm coconut oil can help prevent hair fall.
  • Shikakai: Shikakai is a natural hair cleanser that is gentle on the scalp and helps to prevent hair fall. It is commonly used in Ayurvedic hair care products.
  • Brahmi:Brahmi or Bacopa monnieri is an herb that is used in Ayurvedic medicine for its brain-boosting properties. It is also known to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall.
  • Neem: Neem or Azadirachta indica is an herb that is known for its antibacterial and antifungal properties. It helps to prevent scalp infections that can cause hair fall.
  • Aloe vera: Aloe vera is a natural moisturizer that can help to soothe the scalp and prevent hair fall. It also helps to promote hair growth.
